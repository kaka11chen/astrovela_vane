# Code of Conduct

Vane is committed to a respectful, safe, and productive community.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, technically honest, and open to correction.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Make space for newcomers and people with different backgrounds or communication styles.
- Give credit, preserve authorship, and disclose conflicts of interest.
- Protect private information and redact credentials, datasets, and personal details from public reports.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, and knowingly false claims are not acceptable. Retaliation against someone who reports a concern in good faith is also prohibited.

## Scope

This policy applies in project repositories, issue trackers, reviews, discussions, events, and other spaces where someone is representing Vane.

## Reporting and enforcement

For a public, non-sensitive concern, contact a maintainer listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md). For a confidential or urgent report, use the repository's private security-advisory channel and mark the report as a conduct concern.

Maintainers will limit disclosure to people needed to review the report, give affected parties a fair opportunity to respond when safe, and document the outcome. Responses may include a warning, content removal, temporary restriction, or permanent exclusion. A maintainer involved in a report must recuse themselves; another maintainer or a mutually agreed independent reviewer will handle it.
